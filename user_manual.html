Available Package Types

Packages are configured by your club admin and may vary by city. Each package shows:

  • Total hours included in the package
  • Price (including applicable taxes)
  • Any special terms or member-only restrictions

Hours are credited to your balance immediately after a successful payment.

How Hours Work

  • Each bay session deducts hours from your balance
  • Practice sessions: charged by duration (e.g. 1 hour = 1 hr deducted)
  • Coaching sessions: charged at the coaching rate set per bay — usually higher than practice
  • Unused hours carry over and don't expire automatically
  • View your full hours history on the My Bookings page
If you run out of hours you can still book a walk-in session at the front desk and pay on the spot. Hours packages give you the best value for regular play.

Step by Step

  1. 1Go to Bookings from the navigation menu
  2. 2Your preferred city is pre-selected — change if needed
  3. 3Choose your bay (if multiple bays are available)
  4. 4Pick a date using the calendar picker
  5. 5Available time slots load automatically — they refresh every 30 seconds
  6. 6Choose your session type: Practice or Coaching
  7. 7Select duration (default is 60 minutes)
  8. 8Click Confirm Booking

Session Types

Practice — Standard bay session. Hours deducted = duration ÷ 60. Confirmed immediately.

Coaching — Session with a coach. May require admin approval before confirmation. Hours are charged at the bay's coaching rate (usually higher than practice).

You'll receive a notification when your coaching session is approved or declined.

Coaching sessions show as "Pending" until an admin approves them. Hours are only deducted from your balance after the session is approved.

What You Need

  • Your full name and a valid contact email
  • A valid phone number
  • Payment via Razorpay — accepts card, UPI, or netbanking

No account is required for single one-off sessions.

Differences from Member Booking

  • Payment is required upfront — you cannot use an hours balance
  • No loyalty points are earned for guest bookings
  • Booking confirmation is sent only to the email you enter
  • You cannot view booking history without an account

Your Booking History

Viewing Your Bookings

Go to My Bookings from the navigation menu. Each booking entry shows:

  • Bay name and city location
  • Date and scheduled time
  • Session type: Practice or Coaching
  • Duration and hours deducted from your balance
  • Status badge:
Confirmed Pending Cancelled

Cancellation Policy

You can cancel a confirmed booking directly from My Bookings.

Standard policy: cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before the session start time.

On cancellation:

  • Your hours are automatically refunded to your balance
  • Any loyalty points earned for that booking are reversed
  • You'll receive a cancellation confirmation notification
Cancellation windows may vary — your club admin sets the exact policy. If you need to cancel within 24 hours of your session, contact staff directly at the facility.

Loyalty · Earning

How to Earn Points

Points are awarded automatically — no manual claiming needed for bay sessions.

Bay Sessions

  • Points are awarded automatically after each confirmed session
  • Base rate is multiplied by any applicable bonuses
  • Coaching sessions may earn more points than standard practice sessions
  • Walk-in visits also earn points

Bonus Multipliers

  • Off-Peak Hours — Sessions during off-peak times earn bonus points (e.g. 1.5×)
  • First Visit Bonus — Extra points on your very first session
  • Tier Multiplier — Eagle & Birdie member types may earn at a higher rate

Other Ways to Earn

  • Package renewals for Eagle / Birdie membership plans
  • Milestone completions (see Slide 13)
  • Attending special events that award bonus points
  • Welcome gifts on account creation
  • Check the Earn Methods section on your Rewards page for the full up-to-date list
Points appear in your Rewards page and on your Dashboard shortly after your session is confirmed. If missing after 24 hours, contact staff.

How to Redeem

  1. 1Go to Rewards from the navigation
  2. 2Browse the Rewards Catalogue section
  3. 3Each reward shows its point cost and a description
  4. 4Click Redeem if you have enough points
  5. 5Show the on-screen confirmation to staff when collecting your reward

Things to Know

  • Daily Cap — Some rewards can only be redeemed once per day to prevent abuse
  • Gated Rewards — Certain rewards only unlock after you've used a set percentage of your plan hours (e.g. "Use 50% of hours to unlock")
  • Grey button — Not enough points, or reward is still locked
  • Green button — Available and ready to redeem
Redeemed rewards appear in your Gifted Rewards card with a "Claimed" status once staff confirms the redemption.

Milestones & Gifts

Milestones & Gifted Rewards

Milestones

Milestones are bonus points awarded automatically when you reach certain hours-played thresholds.

Example: "Play 10 hours → earn 500 bonus points"

Your progress toward each milestone is displayed as a progress bar on the Rewards page.

Achieved milestones are marked with a ✓ — the bonus points are credited to your account automatically as soon as you hit the threshold.

  • Multiple milestones can be active at the same time
  • Each milestone can only be achieved once

Gifted Rewards

Admins can manually send you rewards — for example, a free session, a discount voucher, or a merchandise item.

You'll also receive automatic gifts on special occasions, such as when you first sign up.

Check your Gifted Rewards card on the Rewards page:

  • Pending — A Claim button appears. Tap it and show staff to redeem
  • Claimed — Successfully collected
  • Expired — Shown for your records
Milestone progress is based on total hours played across all sessions. Check the Rewards page regularly to see how close you are to your next bonus!

Events

Tournaments & Events

What's Available

Go to Events from the navigation to see all upcoming events at your club.

Event types include:

  • Tournaments — scored competitions with rankings
  • Leagues — ongoing series spanning multiple sessions
  • Social events — fun, non-competitive gatherings

Each event listing shows:

  • Date, time, and location
  • Available spots remaining
  • Registration fee (if applicable)
  • Prizes and rewards on offer

Participating in Events

  • Events are created and posted by your club admin
  • Spots are limited — check availability early to avoid missing out
  • Some events may have a registration fee payable to the club
  • Contact staff or the front desk to register for an event

Upcoming Events

Check the Events page regularly — your admin posts new tournaments, leagues, and social events throughout the season.

Attending certain events may earn you bonus loyalty points. Check the Earn Methods section on your Rewards page to see which events qualify.

Community & Leaderboard

Community Feed

The Community page is your club's social space — a place to connect with fellow members between sessions.

  • Post updates, share game highlights, and celebrate achievements
  • Posts appear in a feed visible to all club members
  • Like and interact with other members' posts
  • Stay engaged with club news and announcements

Keep it friendly and club-focused. Posts are visible to all members at your club.

Leaderboard

The Leaderboard ranks members based on activity and points earned.

  • See where you stand among all members at your club
  • Rankings reflect your sessions played, points earned, and activity level
  • Updated regularly as members play and earn
  • Use the leaderboard as motivation — every session counts!

The more sessions you book and complete, the higher you'll climb. Consistency is rewarded!

Go to Community from the navigation to access both the feed and leaderboard. Both are specific to your club location.

Profile

Managing Your Profile

What You Can Edit

Go to Profile from the navigation menu to view and update your details.

Editable Fields

  • Display Name — the name shown across the app and in the community feed
  • Preferred City — your default location for browsing bays and slots
  • Phone Number — required for booking confirmations and staff contact

Your key stats are also shown on the Profile page: points balance, hours remaining, and your current loyalty tier.

Email Preferences

At the bottom of your Dashboard and Profile, you can manage which email notifications you receive.

Choose your preferences:

  • Booking confirmations & reminders
  • Points earned notifications after sessions
  • Low hours alerts when your balance is running low
  • Event announcements from your club admin

You can opt out of any category at any time without affecting your account.

Keep your phone number up to date — staff use it to contact you for booking changes and facility updates.

Notifications & Emails

Staying Informed

Golfer's Edge keeps you in the loop with both in-app notifications and email alerts for every important event.

Booking Emails

  • Bay Booking Confirmed — slot details, bay name, and your remaining hours balance
  • Coaching Session Pending — your session is awaiting admin approval
  • Coaching Approved — session confirmed with full details
  • Coaching Rejected — with reason provided by admin
  • Booking Cancelled — confirmation with hours refund details

Loyalty Emails

  • Points Earned — after each confirmed session, showing points awarded and your new balance
  • Points Redeemed — confirmation when you redeem a reward from the catalogue
  • Low Hours Alert — notified when your hours balance is running low so you can top up before your next session

Other Notifications

  • In-App Bell — instant alerts for all activity, visible in the notification bell in the app header
  • League & Event Updates — announcements from your club admin about upcoming events
  • Welcome Gifts — automatic notifications on account creation with any gifted rewards
  • Tier Upgrades — when you level up to a new loyalty tier
Manage email preferences on your Dashboard or Profile page. You can turn off any category individually without affecting your account or in-app notifications.

Help & FAQs

Common Questions

Bookings & Hours

How do I cancel a booking?
Go to My Bookings, find the session, and click Cancel. Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before the session start time.
My hours weren't refunded after cancellation.
Refunds are processed automatically. Wait a few minutes, then check your Dashboard balance. If missing after an hour, contact staff at the facility.
Can I book without hours in my balance?
No — you need an hours balance to book online. Purchase a package on your Dashboard, or book a walk-in session directly at the front desk.
Why can I only see certain time slots?
Available slots come from your club's Google Calendar. Slots already booked or blocked by the admin are hidden. Slots refresh every 30 seconds.

Points & Rewards

I didn't get points after my session.
Points appear shortly after your session is confirmed. If they're missing after 24 hours, contact staff with your session details.
Can I transfer points to another member?
No — points are personal and non-transferable. They are tied to your account only.
Why is a reward greyed out?
Either you don't have enough points, or it's a gated reward that requires more plan hours usage before it unlocks. Check the reward description for conditions.
I signed up with Apple and can't see my email.
Go to Profile and edit your email field to replace the Apple relay address (@privaterelay.appleid.com) with your real email address.
Still need help? Contact your club's front desk staff directly — they can resolve most account, booking, and rewards issues on the spot.
